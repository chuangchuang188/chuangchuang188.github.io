About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), supervised by Prof. Xiao-Wu Chen (陈小伍). My research focuses on the Representation Theory of Algebras and Geometry Model of (skew-)gentle algebras.

Research Interests

Representation Theory of Algebras

  • Gorenstein property.
  • Cohen-Macauley Module.
  • Derived Category.

Geometry Model of (skew-)gentle algebras

  • Combinational map.
  • Arf invariant of gentle algebras.
  • Hochschild (co)homology of skew-gentle algebras.
